Frequently Asked Questions

Basic listings remain active indefinitely at no cost. Premium and Featured listings are active for the duration of your subscription. All paid packages are billed monthly and can be canceled at any time. Once canceled, your listing will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

After submitting your business, you'll receive login credentials to our service provider dashboard. From there, you can update your business details, add or remove photos, change service areas, and manage your subscription at any time.

When a potential customer is interested in your services, they can submit an inquiry through our platform. You'll receive these inquiries via email and can also view and manage them in your service provider dashboard. Premium and Featured listings also include a direct contact form on your business page.

All business submissions go through a review process to ensure they meet our quality standards. This typically takes 1-2 business days. We verify that the business exists, that the information provided is accurate, and that any credentials or licenses are valid. Once approved, your listing will go live on our platform.

Yes, you can upgrade your listing package at any time through your service provider dashboard. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your current billing cycle, and the new rate will apply to future billing cycles.
