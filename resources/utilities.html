üí° Save $30-50/Month on Utilities

Winter (Heating)

  • Set thermostat to 68¬∞F (65¬∞F when sleeping)
  • Use space heater in room you're in
  • Close vents in unused rooms
  • Keep curtains closed at night
  • Use draft stoppers on doors

Summer (Cooling)

  • Set AC to 78¬∞F (82¬∞F when away)
  • Use fans to circulate air
  • Close blinds during hottest part of day
  • Cook outside or use microwave
  • Run AC at night when it's cooler

Year-Round

  • Replace all bulbs with LED
  • Unplug devices when not in use
  • Use power strips, turn off when leaving
  • Run dishwasher/laundry only when full
  • Take shorter showers (5-7 minutes)

Internet Savings

  • Buy your own modem/router (pay off in 6 months)
  • Call yearly to renegotiate rate
  • Threaten to cancel for better deal
  • Skip cable, use streaming services
  • Share streaming accounts with roommates
